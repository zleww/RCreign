JOIN THE TEAM

Build your career with RC REIGN MANPOWER AGENCY, a company dedicated to providing excellent recruitment solutions and fostering strong partnerships.

Recruitment Specialist

Sources, screens, interviews, and selects qualified candidates for various job openings. Builds relationships with potential candidates and matches their skills and experience with client requirements.

HR Coordinator

Assists with human resources functions, including employee onboarding, payroll processing, benefits administration, and maintaining employee records.

Account Manager/Business Development Manager

Develops and maintains relationships with client companies, understands their staffing needs, and promotes the agency's services. Identifies new business opportunities and expands the client base.

Operations Coordinator

Supports the day-to-day operations of the agency, ensuring efficient workflow, coordinating with different teams, and resolving operational issues.

Administrative Assistant

Provides administrative and clerical support to the agency staff, including managing correspondence, scheduling appointments, and organizing files.

Payroll Specialist

Processes employee payroll accurately and on time, calculates wages and deductions, and ensures compliance with payroll regulations.

Client Relations Specialist

Focuses on maintaining strong relationships with existing clients, addressing their concerns, and ensuring their satisfaction with the agency's services.
